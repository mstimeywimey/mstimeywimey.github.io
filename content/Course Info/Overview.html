Course time expectations: 125 Hours

Directed Learning - 100 Hours / Self-Directed - 25 Hours

Each Learning Experience (LE) has three organizational components:

  • Learning, Collaborating, and Discussion - 20 hours
  • Electronic Portfolio - 4 hours
  • Reflective Practice - 1 hour

Candidates will engage in the following in each Learning Experience:

ACTIVATE

Initial thinking about the theme and classroom practice - discussion board.

ACQUIRE

Exploring critical research and information on the topic, and sharing new learning on the discussion board.

APPLY

Bringing together new learning in a practical application collaboratively.

CONNECT

Elaborating on learning with Summative Task.

REFLECT

Reflect on new learning.
